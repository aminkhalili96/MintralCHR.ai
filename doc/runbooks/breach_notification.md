# Breach Notification Runbook

## Purpose
Operational checklist for HIPAA-aligned breach notification handling.

## Inputs
- Incident severity classification
- Forensic summary of impacted PHI
- Affected tenant/customer list

## Steps
1. Confirm whether event meets breach-notification threshold with legal/compliance.
2. Determine impacted individuals, data classes, and date range.
3. Prepare customer and internal notification drafts.
4. Execute notifications per contractual and regulatory timelines.
5. Document completed notifications and acknowledgements.

## Minimum Notification Content
- What happened (date/time and detection date)
- What data may be affected
- Mitigations already taken
- Required customer actions
- Contact channel for follow-up

## Evidence
- Legal/compliance approval record
- Notification payloads and timestamps
- Final incident closure memo
